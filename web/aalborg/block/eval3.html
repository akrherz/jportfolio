Web Evaluation III


Background information


You now have had opportunities to experience a few capabilities built into the Global Change course. One capability is the use of the electronic portfolio for managing the student's activities


Tasks for Danish Students


  1. Access the discussion pages in your portfolio and post (as a reply to the thread "Web Evaluation III") your views and opinions on the general design of GC and of the usefulness of web-based electronic portfolio for helping students to manage their learning process.

  2. Reflect on the use of meta-categories for the evolvement of the dialogue. Give your views and opinions on the use of these in terms of enhancing the quality of online collaborative learning and knowledge building

  3. Read comments posted by your classmates and post one comment or more on the opinions expressed by another student.
